VOC & Analytics · Mashreq Bank · Dubai, UAE

Building a VOC Engine from Zero

Feedback cycle reduced from weeks to days
The Challenge

Mashreq Bank had no structured customer feedback infrastructure. Satisfaction data was fragmented across channels, collected manually, and took weeks to reach decision-makers, by which time it was operationally useless. Leadership had no consistent view of CX performance, and product teams were flying blind.

My Process

I first designed a bespoke in-house VOC platform to prove the concept, mapping survey logic, triggers, and reporting architecture from scratch. Once validated, I led the full vendor selection, RFP design, and procurement of Qualtrics, coordinating scoring panels across CX, IT, Legal, and Procurement. Post-implementation, I deployed AI-driven text analytics to auto-categorise open-text feedback at scale. I then built Power BI dashboards that consolidated NPS, CSAT, complaint trends, and sentiment into a single decision-ready view for C-suite.

Tools & Methods
QualtricsPower BI AI Text AnalyticsSentiment Analysis RFP DesignSurvey Architecture SAS Programming
Outcomes
  • Feedback-to-insight cycle reduced from weeks to days
  • Single source of truth for CX performance adopted by C-suite
  • AI-enabled auto-categorisation of open-text feedback at enterprise scale
  • VOC insights directly drove backlog prioritisation across product and digital squads
  • Platform used as primary lens for all quarterly business reviews
NPS & CX Strategy · Mashreq Bank · Dubai, UAE

45-Point NPS Transformation

+45 NPS points · 50% reduction in detractors
The Challenge

NPS scores were declining and end-to-end journey ownership was fragmented across siloed teams. There was no formal governance model, no consistent way to connect customer feedback to delivery decisions, and no mechanism for prioritising journey improvements cross-functionally. Pain points were known anecdotally but never mapped, quantified, or systematically acted upon.

My Process

I developed the bank's first formal CX journey governance model, embedding clear ownership within agile squads and establishing a cross-functional framework for prioritising what mattered most to customers. I applied systems thinking to connect VOC data, survey logic, and comms approval into one coherent end-to-end picture. Journey mapping and discovery workshops identified the highest-impact failure points across HNI, Mortgage, Cards, and Funds Transfer. I led iterative user testing and redesign cycles, presenting journey performance trajectories directly to C-suite and using data to maintain delivery accountability.

Tools & Methods
Service BlueprintingJourney Mapping Agile SquadsVOC Analytics User TestingNPS Governance Power BI
Outcomes
  • 45-point NPS increase over three years
  • 50% reduction in customer detractors
  • Cross-team priority disputes reduced ~30% through governance model
  • CEO's Club Top Contributor: 2019, 2022, and 2023
Digital Transformation · Mashreq Bank · Dubai, UAE

40% Channel Shift in 12 Months

40% reduction in branch & phone demand
The Challenge

Branch and phone channels were absorbing high volumes of avoidable contacts, queries that could be self-served digitally. This was driving operating cost, creating long wait times, and frustrating customers who expected a modern digital experience. Digital channels existed but hadn't earned customer trust.

My Process

I served as CX lead across end-to-end digital journey design for website, mobile banking, chatbot, ATM, CDM, and IVR. I designed and implemented AI-enabled chatbot and self-service journeys, reducing avoidable call volumes and improving first-contact resolution. I co-created 50+ customer educational videos targeting the top FAQ categories. IVR containment rates were analysed and flows redesigned to reduce misrouting. WFM demand data directly informed which digital investments had the highest contact-deflection ROI.

Tools & Methods
Chatbot DesignIVR Architecture WFM AnalyticsUX Prototyping Content StrategyDigital Analytics
Outcomes
  • 40% reduction in branch service demand within 12 months
  • Significant reduction in avoidable call volumes to contact centre
  • 50+ educational videos deployed, reducing inbound query volumes
  • IVR containment rates improved across all banking segments
Strategy & Segmentation · NZ Safety Blackwoods · Auckland, NZ

Uncovering a Hidden $500K Revenue Opportunity

$500K+ annual revenue opportunity quantified
The Challenge

A large B2B portfolio was organised by revenue tier alone, a blunt lens that masked a growing cohort of high-potential accounts quietly disengaging. Leadership had no visibility into which accounts were at risk, which were underserved, or where relationship investment would generate the best return.

My Process

I designed DeciMatrix, a novel multi-factor segmentation model re-categorising accounts across engagement frequency, product breadth, growth trajectory, and strategic fit (beyond revenue). I validated the logic with sales, data, and commercial teams, then ran analysis that pinpointed a previously invisible at-risk segment. A tiered engagement strategy was mapped to each segment, with SOPs for the account management team to execute sustainably. Findings were presented directly to the executive team, securing buy-in for phased rollout.

Tools & Methods
Segmentation ModellingData Analysis BRD WritingSOP Design Executive PresentationCommercial Strategy
Outcomes
  • $500K+ annual revenue opportunity identified and quantified
  • New tiered engagement strategy adopted by account management team
  • Replicable framework established for long-term partnership growth
  • Executive buy-in secured for phased rollout

About

The person behind the work

I didn't start out wanting to be a "CX strategist." I started out wanting to understand why customers were frustrated and then figure out how to fix it. Over 15 years, that question has taken me from contact centres in India to the trading floors of Dubai to the boardrooms of Auckland, and the answer has always been the same: the customer experience is a system problem, not a surface problem.

At Mashreq Bank, where I spent a decade, I learned what it looks like when CX strategy, data infrastructure, and delivery discipline all work together. I helped design the VOC platform from scratch, then watched those insights drive a 45-point NPS turnaround. I built workforce models that informed digital investment decisions. I wrote the BRDs, ran the user tests, managed the vendors, and coached the analysts. Not because I had to, but because I believe the best CX leaders understand every layer.

I've worked inside and alongside CX Centres of Excellence, helping establish the frameworks, governance models, and cross-functional rituals that turn customer insight into consistent business action. I know what it takes to influence decisions at all levels, from product squads to the C-suite, while keeping the voice of the customer front and centre throughout.

"I'm at my best when the problem is complex, the stakes are real, and nobody has quite figured out the answer yet."

These days I'm based in Auckland, bringing that same full-stack approach to organisations ready to treat customer experience as a strategic asset not just a support function. I hold an MBA from Massey University (completed with top academic distinction), a CSPO certification, and SAS programming qualifications.

Outside of work I'm driven by curiosity whether that's exploring how AI is reshaping the CX landscape, picking up new analytical frameworks, or simply talking to customers and discovering what they actually need. That curiosity is what keeps the work honest.
